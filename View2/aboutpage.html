About the project

BookSpace is a platform for reading manga, manhwa, light novels, and comics. It pulls titles from the MangaDex API so you get a broad, up-to-date library without us having to host anything ourselves. You can search by title or genre, bookmark titles to your profile, and download chapters as PDFs to read offline. The goal was something clean and fast — no clutter, just the reading experience.
